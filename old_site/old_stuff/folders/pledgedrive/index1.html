What is it: KCPR's pledge drive is our newly-revived spring quarter fundraiser.  As you know, KCPR is a non-profit community radio station run completely by student volunteers. We rely on the support of our listeners so that we may continue to broadcast our unique programming. We need your help now more than ever to afford station upgrades and maintenance of our equipment. Our goal is to raise $10,000.

Current Pledge Total: $136

When is it: We will officially begin the pledge drive on Saturday, April 16th.  The pledge drive will continue to run from 7:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. until Friday April, 22. Some specialty shows after 6:00 p.m. may also have pledge packages so be sure to also tune into your favorite evening programs.

How it works: You make a pledge over the phone, you get cool stuff.  It's that easy.  We have several KCPR merchandise pledge packages available for $20 and up, including our brand new KCPR Sessions Compilation CD and several new & newly revived KCPR T-shirt designs. In addition, we have a limited supply of items generously donated by local businesses. See below.

How to make a pledge: If you'd like to support KCPR, stop by our booth during Open House or give us a call during pledge drive hours at (805) 756-KCPR. 


Stay tuned to 91.3 FM and KCPR.org for updates
Thank You!
