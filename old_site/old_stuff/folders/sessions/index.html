KCPR Sessions exists to provide local musicians an outlet for radio airplay and as a place for the community at large to hear new music by their friends and neighbors. Each week Sessions features a live, in-studio performance as well as new demo releases and concert updates. Sessions aims to book artists that don't fall into the mainstream of pop music and more or less fit the established programming format of KCPR. If you or someone you know belongs on KCPR, please contact us.

Read the FAQ | MySpace

For the bands:
If you or your band are from the central coast and would like to play, please send us a demo or invite us to a concert or practice session. You can reach the host, Miguel. Please keep your correspondence relevant to Sessions. If you'd like to send us a press kit or your clean radio demo to be played over the air, you may send appropriate information to:

KCPR Sessions
Graphic Arts Bldg 26, Room 301
Cal Poly State University
San Luis Obispo, CA 93407

For the promoters:
If you would like your concert mentioned on the air during KCPR Sessions, you may submit a press release at least two weeks in advance either by email at traffic.kcpr@gmail.com or to our mailing address above. Please limit to general admission shows in San Luis Obispo or northern Santa Barbara Counties.

Please be sure to include:
Names of Performers
Name and Address of Venue
Name of Promoter if applicable
Ticket price and admission restrictions (age/capacity)
Contact phone and/or email address
