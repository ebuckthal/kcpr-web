Each week our music director sifts through numerous albums that have been sent by labels and individual artists. In any given week, the "MD" might listen to as many as 200 albums. Of this, only around 20 albums are added per week. How does the MD complete the daunting task of deciding what is and isn't KCPR material? She applies some music philosophy and some old fashioned Ouija board fun.

Our goal at KCPR is to provide our listeners with great music that typically would not be found on other radio stations. We offer a diverse mix of artists that gives our listeners a chance to hear any type of music. We add certain albums to the station because there is something about it that really enhances the already eclectic variety of our programming.
