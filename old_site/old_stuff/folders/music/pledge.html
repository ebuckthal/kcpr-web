About Pledge Drive
Since 1968, KCPR has been a cornerstone of the San Luis Obispo community, offering a variety of musical, fine arts, news and public affairs programming that otherwise wouldn’t be heard over the limited number of commercial radio stations in town. We are a 100% volunteer, student run non-profit radio station located on the Cal Poly campus. KCPR has no paid positions, and receives no money from the university. We rely on the support of our listeners and community members for the funds needed for our continued operation.

This month, KCPR will be holding its annual pledge drive in which we will ask for donations from members of the community. Next to our annual auction (held in November) this is one of our biggest fundraisers of the year. During the week of May 21st we will be taking donations live on the air between the hours of 10 and 6 p.m. In exchange for listener donations we will be handing out KCPR merchandise along with gift certificates and other small donations from local business owners.

KCPR is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible. Your donation will be described in detail over the air along with the name and address of your business. Our goal is to work in conjunction with local merchants and their donations that positively reflect both the merchant and their support for Cal Poly radio.

Again, KCPR needs your support to make this auction a success! With your help, KCPR can continue to provide our community with the musical variety and public service programming that has been a cornerstone of our philosophy since 1968.
