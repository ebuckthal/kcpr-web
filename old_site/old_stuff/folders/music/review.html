Album Reviews
Our goal at KCPR is to provide our listeners with great music that typically would not be found on other radio stations. And in that spirit would like to provide listeners with Album reviews that typically would not be found on other web sites. We hope to have as diverse reviews as we do adds to the station. The majority of the Albums we review will be new music to the station, although we reserve the right to review an older album that we feel you may have over looked, like a p-cut. All reviews will be made by actual DJ's who play the albums unlike some you find on the web made by promoters trying to sell you the album. We hope this new content will help to inform you on new artist and decide if the album is worth picking up. We here at KCPR encourage you to show your support for up and coming artist and many of the albums we add can be found in town at Boo Boo records.
